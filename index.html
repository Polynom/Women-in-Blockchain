Our cause
As part of our commitment to equal opportunities for everyone, Trust Square together with Procivis and FDP Women Switzerland launched a community fundraiser to benefit young women in Ouahigouya, Burkina Faso. For this purpose, we have built a charity smart contract called “Token of Gratitude” based on which individuals can contribute Ether to the project. In return, the smart contract will issue a token to the contributor’s wallet which will serve as a “donation certificate”. (add stuff about Tulips) The smart contract will run for a total of 8 weeks (August 7, 2018). After this, the funds collected will be released to the wallet of the charity. You can find the repository of the smart contract here (add hyperlink to repo). We believe that fundraising via public ledger can greatly increase transparency and reduce administrative costs for charitable giving. We are very happy that the Swiss charity IAMANEH agreed to collaborate with us to accept donations denominated in Ether.
